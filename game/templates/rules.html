Rules Of Connect 4

  1. The two players take alternate turns dropping one of their discs at a time into an unfilled column
  2. If any of the players connect 4 discs in a row or column or in a diagonal they win
  3. Playing time: 1 - 10 minutes

Rules Of whack the mole

Each player chooses a mole to be his or her mover. Each player then places their mole at the starting position located at the open mineshaft.
If you land on a space with a Hammer Icon on it, you get to whack one of your opponent's moles.
